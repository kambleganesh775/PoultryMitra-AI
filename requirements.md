# PoultryMitra AI - Indian Farm Manager

## Overview
PoultryMitra AI is a farm management platform designed to assist Indian poultry farmers in managing farm operations efficiently using digital tools and AI assistance.

## Functional Requirements

### User Management
- User registration and login
- Profile management
- Role-based access

### Farm Management
- Add and manage poultry farms
- Batch creation and tracking
- Breed classification (Desi and Commercial)

### Poultry Tracking
- Bird count tracking
- Mortality logging
- Egg production records

### Feed Management
- Daily feed consumption tracking
- Feed cost calculation

### Financial Management
- Expense tracking
- Profit and loss calculation
- Revenue reporting

### Health Management
- Vaccination schedule management
- Health alerts and reminders

### AI Assistant
- Chatbot for poultry-related queries
- Disease prevention guidance
- Best practice recommendations

### Reports
- Monthly farm performance reports
- Exportable data

## Non-Functional Requirements

- High availability
- Secure authentication
- Mobile responsive UI
- Data backup
- Fast response time
- Scalability

## Constraints

- Internet connectivity limitations in rural areas
- Budget hardware devices

## Assumptions

- Users possess smartphones
- Basic literacy in local language
